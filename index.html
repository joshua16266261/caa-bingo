Rules

  1. At the beginning of each game, 24 anime titles will be displayed in the Game Area below. Please put them on your board in whatever order you like.
    It doesn't actually matter what you write, as long as it's clear which anime you're referring to.
  2. One at a time, an anime will be randomly chosen from the list. Find it on your board and mark it.
  3. Once you get 5 in a row THREE times, yell "BINGO!".
  4. Have fun and enjoy!
